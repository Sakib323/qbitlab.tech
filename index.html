Our approach involves focusing exclusively on the education sector. 3D
VIEWER


Vitro’s free 3D viewer tool allows students to explore and comprehend 3D models, fostering interactive learning experiences.

The 3D viewer in the Vitro Store is a free tool that students can use to visualize and comprehend 3D models. While the tool includes some models already, students also have the option to download additional 3D models from the internet. This enables them to explore these models in a 3D space, making it an excellent resource for students who wish to learn and delve into intricate concepts through the visual and interactive exploration of 3D models.

AI PDF VIEWER



Our AI-powered PDF viewer streamlines content comprehension by automatically highlighting important points, eliminating the need to sift through the entire text.

Our AI-based PDF viewer enhances your understanding of the content. While you can read the PDF within the viewer, the key feature is its ability to assist you in highlighting important points.This way, you won’t have to go through the entire text to identify crucial information.

See details

NPC 


Virtual NPCs utilize GPT-4 tech to aid students in study routines, responding to queries with lifelike interaction.

Within the virtual world, non-playing characters (NPCs) are created with the purpose of aiding users in their daily study routines. These NPCs utilize GPT-4 technology to provide assistance to students with their studies. Users have the ability to pose any query to the AI NPC avatar, and it will respond accordingly. Additionally, the NPC avatars feature lip syncing and body interaction while engaging in conversation with users.

See details

AI Consultant


Users engage with a trained AI avatar for academic and mental health support, with personalized assistance available through sharing personal details.

User will be directed to a different environment where they can engage with an AI avatar through conversation (our own trained generative ai model) specifically trained to assist students across various subjects and provide support for overcoming depression and anxiety. Sharing some personal details with the AI may enhance its understanding of the issue for more effective assistance.

See details

AI Voice Note


Our AI-powered voice note feature records, transcribes, and summarizes lectures, aiding comprehension and facilitating student engagement through questions.

Students can record their teacher’s lectures using our AI-powered voice note feature. After the lecture ends, the recording will be automatically transcribed into text. Then, AI technology will generate a summary of the lecture, highlighting key points and important details. This summary will help students better understand the topic covered in the lecture. Additionally, students can ask questions about the lecture content, facilitating further engagement and clarification.

Virtual Lab


Dive into the world of chemistry with our immersive VR Chemistry Lab, where students can conduct experiments and explore molecular structures in a virtual environment.

Our VR Chemistry Lab offers an immersive digital environment where students can conduct chemical experiments, explore molecular structures, and visualize complex reactions. Utilizing advanced VR technology, users can interact with virtual equipment, chemicals, and laboratory setups to deepen their understanding of chemistry concepts and principles. From mixing compounds to observing reactions in real-time, our VR Chemistry Lab provides a dynamic and engaging learning experience for students.

F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Vitro and how does it differ from other VR platforms?

Vitro is a revolutionary VR learning platform designed specifically for the educational sector. Unlike other VR platforms that primarily focus on entertainment or gaming, Vitro prioritizes academic enhancement and student engagement. It offers specialized tools and extensions tailored to meet the specific needs of educators and learners, providing a dynamic and interactive educational environment.

  • What makes Vitro VR Education Platform unique compared to other VR platforms?

Vitro stands out from other VR platforms by its exclusive focus on the education sector. Unlike platforms primarily designed for entertainment or gaming, Vitro offers specialized tools and extensions tailored specifically to educators and learners. Our platform prioritizes academic enhancement and student engagement, providing a dynamic and immersive learning environment.

  • Are the tools and extensions on Vitro VR Education Platform (Vitro Store) affordable for students?

Yes, affordability is a crucial aspect of our platform. We recognize that cost can be a barrier to accessing valuable learning resources. Therefore, we strive to offer a range of tools and extensions at affordable prices, and some are even available for free through the Vitro Store. Our goal is to make high-quality educational content accessible to all students, regardless of their financial circumstances.

  • What are the future plans for platform enhancement on Vitro VR Education Platform?

We are committed to ongoing innovation and improvement. In the future, we plan to develop more interactive and immersive content, introduce new features based on user feedback and market trends, and expand into additional sectors such as healthcare, e-commerce, and digital marketing. Our goal is to continuously enhance the Vitro VR Education Platform to meet the evolving needs of educators and learners worldwide.

  • Can students engage with real-time assistance and support on Vitro VR Education Platform?

Yes, our platform offers real-time assistance and support through various features such as the AI Consultant and NPC avatars. Students can engage with AI-driven virtual assistants to receive guidance on academic subjects and support for overcoming challenges like depression and anxiety. Our goal is to provide students with personalized assistance to enhance their learning experience.
